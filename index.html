Meetings

We meet on the first and third Tuesdays of the month. The first is our primary meeting which often includes a speaker. Talks usually last between 30 minutes and 1 hour. Afterwords there's time to network and discuss. The third is a hacknight where we work on personal projects, ask for help, and chat about whatever comes to mind.
